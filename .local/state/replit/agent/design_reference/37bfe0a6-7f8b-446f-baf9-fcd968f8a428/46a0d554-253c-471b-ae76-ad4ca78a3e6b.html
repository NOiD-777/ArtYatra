Warli Art

Maharashtra, India

A traditional tribal art form using simple geometric patterns to depict daily life and nature

Fun Facts:
  • • Uses only white pigment on mud walls
  • • Traditionally painted by women
  • • Over 2,500 years old

Pochampally Ikat

Telangana, India

A resist dyeing technique creating intricate patterns on silk and cotton textiles

Fun Facts:
  • • UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage
  • • Takes 3-4 months to complete one saree
  • • Known as the "Silk City of India"

Thanjavur Painting

Tamil Nadu, India

Classical South Indian paintings characterized by rich colors and surface richness

Fun Facts:
  • • Uses real gold foil and precious stones
  • • Dates back to 16th century
  • • Protected geographical indication status
